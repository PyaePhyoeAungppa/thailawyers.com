BOI Promotion for Waste Treatment or Disposal Project

Thai Lawyers | May 13, 2025

The Board of Investment of Thailand promotes the waste treatment or disposal activities. The project’s income must come from providing waste treatment or disposal services.

In case the project uses a landfill method, only hazardous waste treatment shall be promoted, and the project must receive the approval of environmental impact assessment from the Office of Natural Resources and Environmental Policy and Planning (ONEP) before applying for investment promotion.

Moreover, the project must comply with the following:

  • The waste treatment must meet the standards in the area (e.g., Royal Irrigation Department, Industrial Estate).
  • Must have permission to use the area and a permit to operate wastewater treatment business 101.
  • Main income must come from services; income from extracted products will be a by-product.
  • The machinery must belong to the applicant.

Once promoted, the BOI can grant the following incentives:

  • Corporate Income Tax exemption for 8 years
  • Exemption of import duties on machinery
  • Exemption of import duties on raw materials used in R&D
  • Exemption of import duties on raw materials used in production for export

Non-tax incentives include:

  • Permit to own land
  • Permit to bring foreign skilled workers and experts
  • Permit to bring foreign nationals for investment study
  • Permit to remit funds abroad in foreign currency
